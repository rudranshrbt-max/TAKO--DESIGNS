FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build my salon website?
+
Your complete website is delivered within 7 days of payment and content submission — logo, photos, services list. We'll send you a WhatsApp checklist on day one so we can start immediately.
Do I pay monthly fees after the site goes live?
+
No monthly fees from us. Ever. You'll only pay for your domain (around ₹800–1,200/year) and optional hosting — we set you up on free Netlify or Vercel hosting. Zero subscription traps.
What do I need to provide to get started?
+
Just your salon name, logo (or we create one), 5–10 photos of your salon and work, your services with pricing, and contact details. We handle everything else — design, development, and launch.
Can clients actually book appointments through the website?
+
Yes. Clients choose their service, pick a date and time, and submit their details. You get an instant WhatsApp or email notification. No more back-and-forth DMs or missed bookings.
What if I'm not happy with the final design?
+
You get 2 rounds of free revisions. We also share the design direction before building so there are zero surprises. The balance payment is only collected after you're fully satisfied with the result.
